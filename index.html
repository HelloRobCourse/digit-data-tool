Handwriting Dataset Tool

Use this tool to check your submission for the handwriting dataset. When the status button reads "Complete", you are ready to return to the submission form and submit your images.

Make sure that your images look good when scaled to 28 by 28 pixels. The lines should not be too thick or too thin.

Do your handwriting samples compare to the samples from MNIST? They should take up about the same portion of the image and have similar line thickness.
